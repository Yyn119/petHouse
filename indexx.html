About Us

Group Gorgi was founded in 2023 in Kampar, Perak. Our mission is to save the lives of abandoned dogs and cats and shelter them into a loving and caring homes. We receive mutiples emails everyday to rescue dogs and cats.
Group Gorgi arranges and pays for the medical services such as vaccination and blood tests to ensure the their health. All the pets in Group Gorgi will undergo spayed or neutered at an appropriate age to avoid overpopulation.
Group Gorgi would like to thank the help and support given by the public to foster the homeless pets.
